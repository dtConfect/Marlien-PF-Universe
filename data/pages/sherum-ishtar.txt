====== Sherum-Ishtar ======
Sherum-Ishtar was the founder and first emporer of the [[arthalean_empire|Arthalean Empire]], born around 1,314 [[calendar|BC]] in [[döwer|Döwerslot]], and passing around 1,248 [[calendar|BC]] with the Arthalean conquest ongoing. At his passing, his remains were entombed in a [[mausoleum_of_sherum-ishtar|mausoleum]] overlooking [[Komholt]] from the outcropping of the [[:Hellighil]], accross the [[:River Aflys]].
