====== Arthalean Wastes ======
The Arthalean Wastes are a region of the [[:Paraesi]] continent which occupies the majority of the central western territory controlled by [[:Templar_protectorate_of_arthale]]. It is an uninhabitable stretch of cold, barren, windswept land eastward from the [[:paraesi:cloudcrest mountains]], and south of [[:paraesi:hartcrest]].
