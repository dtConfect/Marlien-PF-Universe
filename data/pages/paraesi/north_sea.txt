====== The North Sea ======
The North Sea of [[:Paraesi]] is a large [[wp>exorheic]] body of salt water located at the north-centre of the [[:Paraesi]] continent of [[:Naltera]]. The source of this vast body of water is the mountains directly northward. Water drains via the [[:north_sea_black_water|Black Water]] at the centre of its southern shores.

===== Surrounding Territories =====
At [[:year zero]], the North Sea is surrounded by the [[:Haleysi]] peoples, and those of the old [[:arthalean_empire]].
