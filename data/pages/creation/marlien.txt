====== Marlien ======
Marlien is a [[creation:magic|magical]] [[creation:creation]] within the [[creation:true_creation:beyond]] which was constructed by the [[creation:marlien:Dead Gods]].
===== Names =====
Marlien is known by many names:
**Marlien** is from the parable of [[religion:solenist_academia|Solen and Marlien]] in the [[:arthale|Arthalean]] religion [[religion:solenist_academia|Solenist Academia]], where it is the name of the creator, or sleeping godess who dreams creation.

===== Topography =====
Marlien is divided into a number of [[planes:planes]] within an outer membrane which bars travel of mortal [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#atman]], [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#mana]], and [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#time]] in or out, while allowing [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#anicca]] to pass in and out, and divine [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#atman]] to enter in. The nature of this membrane is not understood even by the [[creation:true_creation:Charon]] or their counterparts [[creation:true_creation:beyond|beyond]] Marlien.
