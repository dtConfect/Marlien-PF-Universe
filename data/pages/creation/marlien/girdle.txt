====== The Girdle of Marlien ======
The girdle is a semi-permeable membrane of [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#mana]]-imbued [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#anicca]] separating the [[creation:creation]] [[creation:Marlien]] from [[creation:true_creation:beyond]], uniquely structured in such a way that it serves as a [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#trinity|vessel]], but retains a great many [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#atman]].

While it permits the entry of other [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#atman]] it does not permit their exit, nor the extraction of any [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#mana]]. [[creation:true_creation:primordial_elements#Anicca]] is allowed to travel freely into and out of the girdle.
