====== Totum ======
Totum is a non-[[creation:magic|magical]] [[creation:creation]] with many parallels to the [[creation:magic|magical]] [[creation:creation]] of [[creation:Marlien]].

For more information on Totum, see [[wp>universe|its own wiki]], or browse below for specialist topics.

===== Relation of Totum and Marlien =====
The [[creation:creation|creations]] of Totum and [[creation:Marlien]] bear a number of similarities including their order of dimensionality (three dimensions), and the variety of floara, fauna, natural phenomenon, and physics within.
