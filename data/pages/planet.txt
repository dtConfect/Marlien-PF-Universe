====== Planet ======
Planets in the [[creation:creation]] of [[creation:Marlien]] are a rare type of astronomical body, comparable to [[wp>planet]]s in [[creation:Totum]].

The orbital patterns of planets in [[creation:Marlien]] vary greatly, with some locked into orbits with [[spire]]s, others with [[star]]s, and others in more complex arrangements.
