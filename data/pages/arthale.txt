====== Arthale ======
Arthale refers to an area of land on the [[Paraesi]] continent which was once occupied by the [[arthalean_empire|Arthalean Empire]]. This land strentches from the eastern short of the [[paraesi:north_sea|North Sea]], almost to the western shore of the [[dire_sea|Dire Sea]].

At different times the term may also have been used to refer to either the [[arthalean_empire|Arthalean Empire]] itself, or the nations which emerged from its fall.
