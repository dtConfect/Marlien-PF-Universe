====== Gryphon ======
Gryphons are large, intelligent animals with the hind-quarters of a lion, the front part of a great eagle, and a pair of massive, feathered wings.

===== Stats =====
==== Pathfinder ====
=== Vanilla ===
http://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/magical-beasts/griffon

=== Mighty ===
As [[#Vanilla]] but gains the following **Special Abilities**:

== Wingbeat ==
As a full round action, a mighty gryphon may beat its enormous wings once to create a blast of air as the [[http://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/g/gust-of-wind|Gust of Wind]] spell, in a line as wide as the gryphon, and may also move up to half its **fly** speed in any direction other than that of the air blast. The gryphon must have a **fly** speed of at least 20ft to use this ability, and must move at least 10ft.

=== Stormy ===
As [[#Vanilla]] but gains the following **Special Abilities**:

== Static Blast ==
Every 2d4 rounds the gryphon may muster the the power of storms to channel electricity and winds as the spell [[http://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/discordant-blast|Discordant Blast]]. This ability is activated as a standard action, and must be released as another standard action the next round, or forfeit. Between these actions gryphon can move at only half speed, has the benefits of the [[http://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/defensive-shock|Defensive Shock]] spell (CL 1), and the air within ten feet of it, thick with electricity, is considered difficult terrain.

=== Arcane ===
As [[#Vanilla]] but gains two level 1 spontaneous spell slots, and one level 2 spontaneous spell slot per day, with knowledge of  up to three spells from the [[http://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/spell-lists-and-domains/spell-lists---sorcerer-and-wizard#TOC-2nd-Level-Sorcerer-Wizard-Spells|Wizard/Sorcerer spell list]].   * [[http://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/burst-of-radiance|Burst of Radiance]]
