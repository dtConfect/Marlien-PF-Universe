====== Ligeron ======
Ligeron, the Prince of Pain, is a [[:kytonn#interloper|Kyton Interloper]] and subject of [[:Nustalem]], in command of a large part of the [[:Nustalem|god-city]]'s tribes in the eastern part of [[:Paraesi]]. His subjects are the [[:human]] and [[:dhampir]] citizens of his conquered territories, and he is commander of the [[:Nustalem:Daemon_Knights]].
