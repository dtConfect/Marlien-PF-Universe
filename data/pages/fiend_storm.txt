====== Fiend Storm ======
A fiend storm is a classification of [[plane storm]] where one of the planes involved is of a particular nature.

The name has been given to several historical [[plane_storm|plane storms]], including two related events near the [[paraesi:north_sea|North Sea]] of [[Paraesi]].
