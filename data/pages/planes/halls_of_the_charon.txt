====== The Halls of the Charon ======
The Halls of the Charon is a plane carved out of the creation [[creation:Marlien]] by the [[creation:true_creation:Charon#Charon Immortals]]. It entirely encircles the [[planes:Everstair]], barring direct travel to or from that plane.
