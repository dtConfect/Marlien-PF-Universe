====== Emero Wydth ======
Emero Wydth was an ancient city built upon [[:paraesi:north_sea:north_point]], above the [[:paraesi:north_sea]] of [[:paraesi]], by the [[:Emerowydthwaeth]]. Its destruction at the initiation of the [[:first_north_sea_fiend_storm]] marked the total collapse of formal [[:Emerowydthwaeth]] society.
