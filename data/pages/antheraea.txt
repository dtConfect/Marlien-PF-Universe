====== Antheraea ======
Antheraea, the Golden Lady, the Moth Empress, is a [[Charis#Dea]] of the [[Charis]]. She rarely manifests physically, but often appears in visions as a giant woman with rosy skin, golden garments, and an enormous set of yellow moth's wings. Her chief concerns are fortune, charity, and [[creation:true_creation:soul_enrichment]].
