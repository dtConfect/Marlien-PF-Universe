====== Forsaken Darohl ======
Once the singular metropolis of the advanced [[:Darohl]] peoples, the solemn towers and gates hewn from the the rocks at the foothills of [[:Zwo Kayan]] give little indication of damage once inflicted deep within by means of [[:soul_bomb]]s wielded by the rival [[:Graadien]] civilisation in what became known as the [[:Arcane_Holocaust]].
