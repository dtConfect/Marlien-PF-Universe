====== Volcano ======
A volcano is a physical construct found in some [[creation:creation|creations]] consisting of a natural rupture in the crust of a hard, natural, phsyical mass such as a [[spire]] or [[planet]], which allows hot lava, volcanic ash, gases, or other hot matter to escape from a chamber below the surface.

===== Volcanos in Creations =====
==== Marlien ====
Volcanoes in the [[creation:creation]] of [[creation:Marlien]] exist on [[planes:Material]], [[planes:Fire]], and other planes.

On [[planes:Material]] volcanoes occur on [[spire]]s, [[planet]]s and on the main terrain itself, where they are often the route of interesting astrological and astronomic phenomenon including the birth of [[star|stars]].

==== Totum ====
Voclanoes may be found upon [[planet|planets]] in the [[creation:creation]] of [[creation:Totum]] ([[wp>volcano|volcanoes in Totum]]).
