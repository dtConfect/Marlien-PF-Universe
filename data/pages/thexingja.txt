====== Thexingja ======
Thexingja (The-shing-ja) is a planet in [[creation:Marlien]], home of the [[:Xiexio]] people. It is orbits the [[:spire#great spires|great spire]] [[:Dajoxin]] at such a distance as to allow it to also be locked in orbit with the [[:star]] [[:Kwonyen]], and possesses several moons.

The kinesis-reactive mineral [[:Jinxi]] is found naturally occurring on Thexingja, its moons, and several nearby planets.
